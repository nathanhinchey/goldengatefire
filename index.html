A trusted name in fire safety

When it comes to fire protection, you don't want to be unprepared. That's why Golden Gate Fire Equipment has been helping keep the Bay Area safe for over 25 years. We provide affordable, convenient service all over the bay area. We do residential, commercial, and industrial locations. With Golden Gate Fire Equipment, you never have to come to us — all of our service is mobile.

Whether you're looking for training, need maintenance for your existing fire extinguishers, need an inspection for your fire hoses, or need new fire safety equipment, Golden Gate Fire Equipment is here for you. See our contact information here.

Training

Being prepared is about more than just having the right equipment. If you want your workplace to be better prepared in case of an emergency, we are available to lead training sessions. Typically our training involves a discussion of fire code, proper ways to respond in case of a fire, and an opportunity to practice discharging a fire extinguisher.

We have taught fire extinguisher usage lessons to offices, warehouses, and even at city wide safety fairs. To request a training, contact us!
